Our Story

Two Generations of Craft

In 1982, George and Nawal Garabet — two Lebanese immigrants in pursuit of the American Dream — opened Lara's Jewelry with the help of their 15-year-old son, Roger.

For over 40 years, Lara's has been devoted to providing love and happiness through high-quality jewelry. Today, Roger is joined by his brother, Georgie, to carry on the tradition started by their parents.

Anyone who walks through our doors is treated as a part of our family.

As a diamond importer and manufacturer, Lara's carries a large inventory of GIA-certified loose diamonds. Every piece is carefully handcrafted in their Los Angeles shop on the fifth floor of the International Jewelry Plaza.

Custom Jewelry Design

Los Angeles Custom Jewelry Design

We design custom jewelry that's as breathtakingly unique as your story.

Whether for a gift, engagement, proposal or for yourself, custom jewelry design is a special way to commemorate a person or event in your life by marking it with a custom diamond design that will become a family heirloom. Throughout the decades, we've become experts at custom jewelry design in Los Angeles, and we know how to create pieces that will make you fall in love all over again.

The Process: How it Works

01

Inspiration

Before we can get started, you should send us pictures that help visualize your custom jewelry design idea.

02

Design

Next, we will work on a custom jewelry design that incorporates the style you want and takes your inspiration into account.

03

Rendering

Afterwards, we will send you sketches or renders of how we expect the piece will look once we are finished.

04

Completion

Finally, we will build and ship your custom jewelry. You can also pick it up from our store in Los Angeles, California.
